Payment strategy

Place fixed obligations in the first half of the month and keep variable obligations in post-paycheck windows. This lowers short term volatility while preserving flexibility.
  • Keep two manual payment windows: Day 5 and Day 20.
  • Trigger review alerts when upcoming obligations exceed 35% of available liquid balance.
  • Prefer AutoPay on utilities and subscriptions, keep debt payments manual for timing control.
