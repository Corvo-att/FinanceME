**FinanceME**

**🏠Core/Navigation Pages**

* **Landing Page** — Marketing page for new visitors with feature highlights, pricing, and CTAs to sign up or log in.  
* **Dashboard (Home)** — The main hub after login. Shows a summary of net worth, recent transactions, budget status, account balances, and key charts.

🔐Authentication Pages

* **Sign Up / Register** — Email, password, optional OAuth (Google, Apple). May include onboarding steps.  
* **Log In** — Standard login form with "remember me" and forgot password link.  
* **Forgot Password / Reset Password** — Email-based recovery flow.  
* **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** — TOTP or SMS verification step during login.

💳 Accounts & Balances

* **Accounts Overview** — List of all linked or manual accounts (bank, credit card, investment, crypto, cash).  
* **Account Detail Page** — Transaction history, balance over time, and account-specific stats for a single account.  
* **Add / Edit Account** — Form to manually add or edit an account (name, type, currency, balance).  
* **Link Bank Account** — Integration flow (e.g., Plaid) to connect a real bank account.

💸 Transactions

* **Transactions List** — Full paginated, filterable, sortable transaction history across all accounts.  
* **Transaction Detail** — View/edit a single transaction (amount, category, notes, attachments).  
* **Add / Edit Transaction** — Manual entry form for income or expenses.  
* **Import Transactions** — Upload CSV/OFX/QIF files from a bank export.  
* **Recurring Transactions** — Manage subscriptions and repeating income/expenses.

📊 Budgets

* **Budgets Overview** — All active budgets with progress bars showing spending vs. limits.  
* **Budget Detail** — Breakdown of a specific budget category with transaction drill-down.  
* **Create / Edit Budget** — Set spending limits by category, time period, and rollover rules.

🎯 Goals & Savings

* **Goals Overview** — List of all financial goals (emergency fund, vacation, home down payment).  
* **Goal Detail** — Progress tracker, contribution history, and projected completion date.  
* **Create / Edit Goal** — Define target amount, deadline, and linked account.

📈 Reports & Analytics

* **Spending Analysis** — Breakdown of expenses by category, merchant, or time period with charts.  
* **Income vs. Expenses** — Monthly/yearly comparison of money in vs. money out.  
* **Net Worth Over Time** — Historical chart of total assets minus liabilities.  
* **Cash Flow Report** — Monthly cash flow statement.  
* **Category Trends** — How spending in specific categories has changed over time.  
* **Tax Summary** — Aggregated view of deductible expenses and income for tax purposes.

🗓️ Planning & Forecasting

* **Bill Calendar** — Calendar view of upcoming bills and scheduled payments.  
* **Cash Flow Forecast** — Projected balance over the next 30/60/90 days.  
* **Debt Payoff Planner** — Snowball or avalanche method calculators for loans and credit cards.  
* **Retirement / Investment Projector** — Long-term wealth projection based on current contributions.

🔔 Notifications & Alerts

* **Alerts & Notifications Page** — List of all triggered alerts (low balance, budget exceeded, large transaction).  
* **Manage Alert Rules** — Create/edit custom alert conditions (e.g., "notify me if spending exceeds EGP 500 in dining").  
* **Subscriptions** — For long-unused and forgotten subscriptions that drain the user’s money

⚙️ Settings & Configuration

* **Profile Settings** — Name, email, avatar, password change.  
* **Security Settings** — 2FA setup, active sessions, and login history.  
* **Notification Preferences** — Email, push, and in-app notification toggles.  
* **Connected Accounts / Integrations** — Manage linked banks, payment apps, and third-party services.  
* **Categories Manager** — Create, rename, merge, or delete transaction categories.  
* **Currency & Locale Settings** — Default currency, date format, and number format.  
* **Subscription / Billing** — Manage plan, view invoices, update payment method.  
* **Data Export** — Download all financial data as CSV, PDF, or JSON.  
* **Delete Account** — Account closure flow with data wipe confirmation.

🛠️ Admin Pages *(if multi-user or SaaS)*

* **User Management** — View, search, suspend, or delete user accounts.  
* **Analytics Dashboard** — Platform-level stats (signups, active users, revenue).  
* **Support Tickets** — View and respond to user-submitted issues.  
* **Feature Flags** — Toggle beta features on/off per user or globally.

📋 Misc / Support Pages (OPTINAL)

* **Onboarding Wizard** — Step-by-step setup after first sign-up (add accounts, set budgets, set goals).  
* **Help Center / FAQ** — Searchable knowledge base and how-to guides.  
* **Changelog / What's New** — Release notes for new features.  
* **404 / Error Pages** — Friendly error states for not found, server errors, etc.  
* **Terms of Service & Privacy Policy** — Legal pages.
